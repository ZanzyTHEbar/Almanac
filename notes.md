# Notes for development

## What should be in a gardening journal?

1. Date
2. Weather
    - Temperature
    - Rain
    - Wind
    - Frost
3. What was done
4. What was planted (and when)
5. What was harvested

## What should be in a gardening calendar?

- What to plant
- When to plant
- When to harvest
- When to prune
- When to fertilize
- When to water
- When to weed
- When to mulch
- When to protect from frost
